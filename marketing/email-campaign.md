## Email Best Practices

1. **Subject Lines**: 
   - Keep under 50 characters
   - Use numbers, questions, or urgency
   - Avoid spam words

2. **Personalization**:
   - Use first name
   - Reference past interactions
   - Segment by interest

3. **Mobile Optimization**:
   - Short paragraphs
   - Large buttons
   - Single column layout

4. **Clear CTAs**:
   - One primary action
   - Prominent button
   - Clear benefit

5. **Timing**:
   - Tuesday-Thursday, 10 AM - 2 PM
   - Avoid Mondays and Fridays
   - Test different times

6. **A/B Testing**:
   - Subject lines
   - Send times
   - CTA text
   - Content length
